Our Story

Where Rustic Charm Meets Luxury

Perched high on handcrafted wooden machaans, Machaan Countryside transforms a simple highway stop into an unforgettable dining destination.

15-20 km from Rewa

The Machaan Experience

Imagine dining 12–15 feet above the ground on beautifully crafted elevated wooden platforms, surrounded by lush greenery with panoramic countryside views stretching to the horizon.

Located on Allahabad Road (NH 30), Raipur Karchuliyan, near Rewa — we're the perfect highway getaway for families, couples, and food lovers seeking something extraordinary.
